Cyberjaya Development Council

The Cyberjaya Development Council is a network comprising of agencies and industry players that work together to develop initiatives to support the successful implementation of initiatives that will elevate Cyberjaya into becoming a Global Tech Hub.

The council provides valuable inputs in the form of perspective, experience, best practices, and marketing activities and coordinate the implementations of the key enablers.

The council recommends the incentives for policy formulators to attract companies to Cyberjaya including tax benefits and financial grants.

Talent programmes are developed to address gaps in talent, provide incentives to attract niche expertise and establish communication streams with universities on latest technology development.

To enable research and development, available technology platforms that can be leveraged are identified to expand on research and development programmes while identifying leads to prioritize on impactful initiatives.

SMEs are given support as the council establishes networks with venture capital and investment companies, while identifying available funding support for SMEs and improving their infrastructure with relevant parties.
