Tan Sri Dr. Mohd Irwan Serigar Abdullah Chairman

Tan Sri Dr. Mohd Irwan Serigar Abdullah

Chairmain

Tan Sri Dr. Mohd Irwan Serigar Abdullah, assumed the position of the Chairman on 1 November 2012.

Tan Sri Mohd Irwan holds a B.A. (Hons) Population Studies from University of Malaya, Kuala Lumpur in 1982, a M.Sc. in Energy, Management and Policy from University of Pennsylvania, Philadelphia, USA in 1994 and a Ph.D in Economics from International Islamic University, Kuala Lumpur in 2003. He was appointed to the Malaysian Administrative and Diplomatic Service (PTD) as Assistant Director, Regional Economics Division Economic Planning Unit, Prime Department, Kuala Lumpur in 1984. Tan Sri Mohd Irwan is still in the service, currently serving as Secretary General of Treasury, Ministry of Finance, Malaysia.

Aside from Cyberview Sdn Bhd, Tan Sri Mohd Irwan is also the Chairman for Kumpulan Wang Amanah Pencen (KWAP) and Chairman of Inland Revenue Board of Malaysia among others.
